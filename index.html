FAQ

Common Questions

Is VivahBiodata completely free?
Yes, 100% free forever. No hidden fees, no premium plans, no subscription. Create unlimited biodatas, download unlimited PDFs — always free. No credit card, no signup.
Does my data get stored anywhere?
Absolutely not. Everything runs 100% in your browser. Your form data, your photo, and the generated biodata never leave your device. No servers. No database. Close the tab and everything is gone — by design.
Can I add "Shree Ganeshay Namah" or other religious invocations?
Yes! This is one of our most loved features. We have 12+ presets: Hindu (ॐ श्री गणेशाय नमः, ॐ नमः शिवाय, श्री राम जय राम, जय माता दी, etc.), Muslim (Bismillah), Christian (Holy Trinity), Sikh (Waheguru), Jain (णमो अरिहंताणं), and a custom text option for any other invocation.
How does the photo crop tool work?
Simply click the photo section and upload any image. A crop modal pops up automatically showing a circular frame. Drag the image to reposition your face, use the zoom slider (or pinch on mobile) to scale. Click "Apply Photo" and the perfect circular photo appears in your biodata instantly.
Which template should I choose?
All 6 are beautiful — choose based on your preference: Marigold (traditional Hindu), Navy (modern professional), Rose (feminine/elegant), Saffron Royal (palatial/heritage), Peacock Emerald (exotic/distinctive), Midnight Luxe (dark/luxury). You can switch anytime without losing your data.
Can I use this on my mobile phone?
Yes, fully mobile-responsive. The form, photo cropper, preview, and PDF download all work on Android and iPhone. The crop tool even supports pinch-to-zoom on touchscreens.
What fields does the biodata include?
A very comprehensive set: Name, DOB, time/place of birth, height, weight, build, complexion, blood group, marital status, mother tongue, languages, hobbies, lifestyle (diet/smoke/drink), religion, caste, sub-caste, gotra, 27 nakshatras, rashi, manglik, charan, education, occupation, income, work city, parents, siblings, family type (joint/nuclear), family status, native place, NRI status, maternal uncle, contact details, and about me + partner preferences.
